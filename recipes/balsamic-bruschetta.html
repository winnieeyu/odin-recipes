Balsamic Bruschetta

Description

A 5-star recipe for Balsamic Bruschetta that shows you how to bump up that basic tomato mixture with basil, balsamic vinegar, garlic, and Parmesan cheese; and how to toast the bread in the oven.

Ingredients

  • Load of French bread
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
