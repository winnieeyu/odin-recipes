Peach Cobbler

Description

A warm mixture of sweetened, juicy peaches topped with a biscuit-like dough and sprinkled in cinnamon sugar. A simple recipe that comes together in just one hour.

Ingredients

  • Fresh peaches
  • White sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Ground cinnamon
  • Ground nutmeg
  • Fresh lemon juice
  • Cornstarch
  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Unsalted butter
  • Boiling water
